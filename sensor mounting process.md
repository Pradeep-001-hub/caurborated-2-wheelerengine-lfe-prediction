STEP 1: Find exhaust pipe
        30-40cm from engine head

STEP 2: Mark drilling point
        On SIDE of pipe
        Never bottom (condensation)
        Never top (heat pocket)

STEP 3: Take to fabricator
        Tell him:
        "Weld M18x1.5 bung here"
        Cost: ₹100-150

STEP 4: After welding + cooling
        Apply anti-seize paste
        on sensor threads

STEP 5: Screw in LSU 4.9
        Hand tight first
        Then 40-50 Nm torque

STEP 6: Route cable AWAY
        from hot pipe surface
        Use cable ties every 10cm
        Add heat sleeve where
        cable passes near pipe
